My name is Natasha Goodsell, I’m a 20-year-old artist living in Sydney Australia. I grew up loving to draw characters from movies, shows and books that I loved. Eventually, the sketches I loved to draw turned into full-coloured drawings when I reached high school. I did visual arts and design and technology in years 11 and 12 where I created my first picture book for my major work. I began drawing more and more, especially during the COVID-19 lockdown when I started getting notices from companies like DreamWorks who have reposted my drawings of characters from their movies. I then decided to pursue this further by studying for a degree in Visual Arts and Design minoring in Digital Technologies at the Australian Catholic University (ACU) in Strathfield. This year I volunteered at the Biennale of Sydney which was a great experience to interact and make connections with other artists and build upon my communication skills. I have also been commissioned to create illustrations of backdrops for a school musical production and have designed a website for a small business. I have also been a part of ACU exhibits in 2022 and 2023 along with The Local exhibit this year. I’m in my third year working on a new picture book for my final major work for my degree. This degree has also helped me gain skills in graphic design which I wish to pursue after I finish my degree.
