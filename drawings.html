Coloured Prismacolor drawings of animated characters on Strathmore toned gray paper

drawings from left to right, top to bottom: Poppy from Dreamwork's Trolls, Toad from Illumination's Super Mario Bros. Movie, Branch from Dreamwork's Trolls, Sid from Blue Sky's ice age, Alberto from Pixar's Luca, Flynn from Disney's Tangled, Marlin and Dory from Pixar's Finding Nemo, Olaf from Disney's Frozen, Wade from Pixar's Elemental.

I have been drawing animated characters for almost my entire life and I suppose it is a form of fan art, recreating 3D animated characters on a 2D surface. I love to challenge myself and bring alive the characters, alot of the time I exaggerate colours and alter features slightly to make it look good on paper. I use Prismacolor Pencils which are very pigmented pencils almost crayon like that makes blending easy and allows the drawings to really pop. The toned gray paper also really makes the colour stand out along with my favourite jellyroll white gell pen i use for highlights!

Coloured Prismacolor Portraits on Strathmore toned gray paper

Portraits spanning from 2022-2024

Drawing animated characters for a long period of time has really influenced my cartoon like style in more realistic areas of my art practice. I love to draw portraits but also snapshots of human body parts, drawing them on larger scales to what they are in real life which really allows me to bring out details I would not be able to achieve otherwise.

Printmaking assignments 2023

Jellyshrooms, 2023, ink and Prismacolor Pencil on Strathmore Paper

These prints delineate the importance of respecting and understanding both sea and land life through the representation of jellyfish and mushrooms. By using metamorphosis and colour, the connection between different life forms is evident which is further amplified through the use of printmaking and drawing techniques.

Flying Saucers, 2023, ink on paper

This work is an experimention of printing focusing on imagry of eyes through screenprinting text, drawings and photographs.
