Design for Publication 2023

Pages and design for ficitional Vantage Magazine 2022

In a unit called design for publication we made a magazine as a class for a fictional gallery called Australian Design Museum. This is my proposed cover and a mock up layout of my style guide that I pitched to the class. The Class was split into teams to produce the magazine. I was part of the editorial team who curated the articles. The art direction team selected a students style guide for the class to follow. Each student had to write up and design four pages for the magazine. This is the article I wrote and pages I designed for the magazine using the nominated style guide.
