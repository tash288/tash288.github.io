Recent Events

Knock Knock?, 2024, pencil, watercolour and gouache on paper

My most recent work, Knock Knock?, swirls with portraits of myself connecting and interacting with each other. Bursts of contrasting colours and changing materials of pencil, watercolour and gouache paint, depict the versions of myself I feel I show in different times and places among varying company. I want to symbolise how our identity and personality can feel as if it is always in flux through distorting the face, the most expressive part of the human body. Within a world where society deems we as individuals must fit a certain mould, I want to communicate that it's okay to not always know who you are.
