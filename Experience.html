Illustrations for St Patricks Collage Campbelltown School Musical

Wizard of Oz Illustrations

I was commissioned by St Patrick's College Campbelltown to draw backgrounds for their school musical production of Wizard of Oz. The backdrops I drew were based on descriptions given to me by the art production team. Some of these images were also animated, using procreate, in some backdrops I would draw in a bird flying past, the flames were animated to move, the windmill went around in a circle etc

Volunteering at the Biennale of Sydney 2024

Art Exhibit at the White Bay Power Station

I spent 3 months volunteering at the Biennale of Sydney for over 110 hours. My tasks there included welcoming visitors, collecting feedback helping visitors interpet and connect to the art though converstations, helping ensure saftey of artworks and offering general information and directions.
